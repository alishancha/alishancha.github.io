About us.

Excellence in every cup is what you can expect when you visit Cafe Sapientia. We are your premier coffee shop in Oak Park, CA, dedicated to serving the highest quality single origin-based coffee drinks and much more. When your palate is craving the delectable flavors of premium gourmet coffee drinks, stop by our shop today and see what is calling your name.

We're much more than your average coffee shop. Located in the center of Oak Park, at 706 Lindero Canyon Rd, we're all about elegant presentation and exquisite tastes. We're passionate about coffee, and we're excited to share our love of specialty brews with you. Our shop is a dynamic space where you can sit and sip your favorite drink, do homework or work, or spend time catching up with friends. Our goal is for you to have a thoroughly enjoyable experience.

How it all began.

Two coffee and tea enthusiasts who are childhood best friends opened their own cafe in beautiful Oak Park neighborhood to serve best quality drinks and special Korean style shaved ice, Snow Creme.
